# What dashboard Does

## In Simple Terms
A home page for your Q03 server. Open it on your phone or PC to see
at a glance whether your services are running, how much RAM and disk
the server is using, and quick links to open each service.

## Who It Is For
bumble_q — anyone who wants to check on Q03 without SSHing into it.

## How to Use It
1. Open http://100.101.233.78:8082 (works from any Tailscale device)
2. Green dot = service is up. Red dot = service is down.
3. Tap a service card to open it.
4. Stats update every 10 seconds automatically.
